Our Story

LiveTrack Pro was born from a simple frustration — businesses were losing customers because they couldn't see who was visiting their website or engage with them at the right moment.

Founded in 2024, we set out to build the most intuitive visitor tracking and live chat platform. We believed that every business, regardless of size, should have access to powerful tools that help them understand and connect with their website visitors in real-time.

Today, LiveTrack Pro powers thousands of websites worldwide, helping businesses track millions of visitors and handle hundreds of thousands of conversations every month.

Our Mission

To democratize real-time customer engagement. We believe every business deserves to know who's visiting their website, what they're looking for, and the ability to engage them instantly — without complex setups or expensive tools.

What We Believe

Simplicity First

Powerful tools shouldn't be complicated. LiveTrack Pro can be set up in under 2 minutes with a single line of code. No training manuals, no consultants, no headaches.

Real-time Matters

In today's fast-paced world, delayed responses mean lost customers. Our WebSocket-powered infrastructure ensures zero-delay messaging and instant visitor tracking.

Privacy by Design

We track visitor behavior to help businesses serve better — not to exploit data. We're GDPR compliant, we don't sell data, and we give businesses full control over what they collect.

Accessibility for All

Our Free plan isn't a teaser — it's a fully functional product. We believe core visitor tracking and live chat should be accessible to startups, small businesses, and enterprises alike.

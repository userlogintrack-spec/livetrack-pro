Features

177+ Features Built for
Modern Support Teams

Everything you need to track visitors, engage customers, and scale your support — all in one platform.

Real-Time Live Chat

WebSocket-powered instant messaging. File sharing, image preview, emoji support, typing indicators, and read receipts — zero latency.

Visitor Tracking & Analytics

See every visitor in real-time — IP, browser, OS, device, location (country/city), pages visited, referrer source, and time on site.

Customizable Widget

Match your brand — custom colors, title, position. Live preview as you design. One-line embed code works on any website.

Multi-Agent & Teams

Add unlimited agents. Auto-assign chats with least-busy routing. Transfer between agents. Whisper mode for internal collaboration.

Analytics Dashboard

Hourly visitor charts, browser/device breakdown, traffic sources, agent performance stats, CSAT ratings, and SLA monitoring.

Canned Responses & Shortcuts

Create reusable replies. Type / to instant-search and insert. Quick reply chips for common scenarios. Save hours daily.

Smart Notifications

Desktop push notifications, email alerts for new chats, unread count in browser tab, sound alerts — never miss a message.

Proactive Chat Triggers

Auto-popup chat invitations after custom delay. Configurable message. Engage visitors before they leave your site.

Webhooks & Integrations

Connect to any system with webhooks. Events for chat created, closed, new message. HMAC signature for security. Slack-ready.

Multi-Tenant & Secure

Each account is fully isolated. WebSocket token auth, CSRF protection, rate limiting, visitor banning, and role-based access.

Business Hours & Offline

Set working hours — widget auto-switches to offline mode. Custom offline message. Offline form collects leads while you sleep.

Complete Audit Trail

Activity log tracks every action — who joined, transferred, closed chats. Chat transcripts downloadable. Full message archive.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is LiveTrack Pro really free?
Yes! Our Free plan is completely free forever — no credit card required. It includes visitor tracking, live chat, and basic analytics for up to 100 visitors per month.
How do I add the chat widget to my website?
Copy the embed code from your dashboard and paste it before the closing </body> tag. It works with any platform — WordPress, Shopify, Wix, React, or plain HTML.
How many agents can use the platform?
Free includes 1 agent, Pro includes 5, and Enterprise offers unlimited agents. All can handle multiple chats simultaneously.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow industry-standard security practices and are GDPR compliant. Your visitors' data is never shared with third parties.
Can I customize the chat widget?
Yes! Customize the widget color, welcome message, and position from the admin panel. Pro and Enterprise plans support custom branding and white-labeling.
What's your refund policy?
14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund — no questions asked. See our Refund Policy for details.
Do you offer customer support?
Yes! Free gets email support, Pro gets priority email + chat, and Enterprise gets a dedicated account manager with 24/7 phone support.

What Are Frustration Signals?

Rage Click
User rapidly clicks the same element multiple times — a sign of frustration with unresponsive UI.
Dead Click
A click that produces no response — the element looks clickable but doesn't do anything.
Quick Back
User navigates to a page and immediately returns — content didn't match expectations.
Excessive Scroll
User scrolls up and down repeatedly — they can't find what they're looking for.
